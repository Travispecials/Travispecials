ABOUT



Travis is currently final year student in City University of Hong Kong where he is studying Information Management in Internet services and social network stream. He has worked in Madrid for an online marketing and design internship.

People describe him as: social, outgoing, energetic, and active; yet still a prudent leader that gets the job done. He has taken up many leadership roles in Extra- Curricular Activities like the Information Systems Society, which serve to demonstrate my talents and skills.

Outside of work and studying, Travis enjoys exploring the city and taking photos. He always thinks that life should be an adventure because we live in a wonderful world that is full of beauty, charm and there is no end to the adventures that we can have if only we seek them with our eyes open.

He is adept and qualified in the digital marketing and social media field, and he continuing to pursue more knowledge in these fields, in order to keep improving, and further display his skills.
